Anyone above 18 years old with a valid driving license can register.
▾ Do I need to bring my own bike?

Yes, the beginner-level course is completely free thanks to our sponsors.
▾ What will I learn in the course?
You'll learn bike control, safety protocols, basic maintenance, and traffic rules.
▾ Do I need to have a bike to join the free learning sessions?
Bikes and helmets are provided for training during the sessions. (first come first serve basis )
▾ Are certificates provided after the course?
Yes, you'll receive a certificate of participation on successful completion.
▾ Are there different race categories?
Yes, races are divided by engine capacity and rider experience (Beginner, Intermediate, Pro).
